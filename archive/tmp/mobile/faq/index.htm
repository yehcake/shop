FAQ 常見問題

Product

How should your cakes be stored?

Our cakes are delivered fresh and are best served on the day of delivery. If that turns out not to be the case, the cakes should be refrigerated and then later enjoyed at room temperature.

Can you adjust your recipe for special dietary requirements?

Our custom cakes are just that -- custom! So we're happy to entertain any design and taste request, for instance. In some cases, we have practical limitations. However, we have made cakes with special dietary restrictions in the past, and we're happy to work with you on your requirements.

How do your different layer cakes compare in size?

The height of our (non-custom) layer cakes are all the same, namely an industry standard 10 cm (4 in). They simply differ in diameter. You can visualize the difference here.

How should I cut my cake?

Typical wedge cuts don't really work for large cakes because you end up with giant slices of cake. An alternate approach to cutting a larger (round) cake, which we recommend, is to cut rectangular pieces. You can visualize the technique here.

Can I eat the flowers, figurines, etc. decorating your cakes?

We'd personally recommend that you fill your appetite on the cake itself. While the flowers and figurines, for instance, are technically edible, we don't think you'd really enjoy the taste. Further, some of our more complex designs require structural support. So you wouldn't want to inadvertently ingest a piece of uncooked pasta. In short, we recommend you stick with the cake!

Ordering

How far in advance do I need to place an order?

We typically suggest at least one week notice. Depending on the type (e.g., custom) and timing (e.g., holiday) of your order, it may be prudent to reserve well ahead of time to ensure we can schedule your order.

How can I place my order?

For fastest processing, we suggest you place your order online. If more convenient for you, you can also call, text, email or fax us your order.

What happens after I submit my order?

Once we review your order, we will email you an order confirmation, thus it is important that you provide an email address on all orders. Please note your order has not been processed until you receive an affirmative confirmation from us.

Can I order a half-dozen cake pops only?

In some instances, it is difficult to prorate a recipe as, for instance, one cannot halve an egg. Therefore, our minimum order size is occasionally constrained by our recipes. In other words, this is a long way of saying that we don't typically accept orders for only a half-dozen cake pops.

Do you have a storefront?

Presently, we operate on a catering / delivery basis so we have no storefront address. If you are looking to place an order, please contact us.

Payment

How do I pay you?

Kindly pay cash on delivery.

Delivery

What is your service area?

Our principal service area is Taipei City. However, if your need is outside of this area, please contact us and we'll try our best to accomodate you.

How much is delivery?

Within the Tianmu area, delivery is free. Outside of that, delivery is NT$210 per order.

When will you deliver my order?

We always do our best to deliver your order when requested. Occasionally, for practical reasons, we will deliver your order ahead of schedule -- up to a day before. For instance, if your requested delivery time is Sunday at 8am, we will likely deliver your order on Saturday. However, we will always notify you in these extreme cases.

Can you notify me before you arrive?

At this time, if you provide us with a mobile number and request as such, we would be happy to SMS you ahead of our arrival.

Service

Do you ever offer special discounts?

From time-to-time, we offer special discounts. To receive notification of such offers, please "like" us on Facebook.

What do I do if there is a problem with my order?

We want happy customers! So please contact us as soon as you discover the problem and we'll go from there.

Technical

What are your site's browser requirements?

Our site has been tested with the latest desktop versions of Internet Explorer, Safari, Firefox and Chrome so using any one of these web browsers should afford the full experience of our site. Additionally, your browser must be configured to accept cookies and use JavaScript, else our web site will not function properly.

Is your site secure?

All information sent to us is transmitted over SSL (i.e., encrypted). Additionally, we've outsourced the storage of our data to Google. So we believe, in these regards, our security practices are at least comparable to the majority of e-commerce sites out there today.

How do I report a problem with your site?

Our apologies with your difficulty. We try our best to offer a reliable and robust experience on our web site. However, if you encounter a problem, kindly email us at info@yehcake.com. We would appreciate as much detail as you can provide.
